What's in it for your inustry:

Retail

In a retail environment - whether it is a stand-alone store or a multi-location network - the efficiency of your labour force depends on how accurately you are able to forecast number of transactions for any given period of time. Simply upload your historical transaction data and decide how to predict the forecasted period by adjusting either same period of last month, same period of last year, average of the same calendar days for the previous x number of weeks. Another important factor is to allocate appropriate time for all the non-transaction related daily/weekly/monthly tasks. Our rota planning solution is designed in a way, that every such period starts with the pre-defined base workload amount and whenever staff is not fully occupied with serving customers, the base workload time gets allocated, so you can make sure that you always have the right amount of staff to serve customers and finish all other tasks.
